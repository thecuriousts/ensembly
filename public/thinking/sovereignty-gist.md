## One line

**Sovereignty is the alpha:** own the value you create and the freedom to pursue new opportunity — especially the *context flywheel* of knowhow — while treating frontier models as liquid commodities, not landlords of your intelligence.

---

## Stack (own the top)

| Layer | Role | Public rule |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Control** | Workflows, ontology, agents, audit, branching | Own it. Model-agnostic. |
| **Models** | Rented closed or owned open weights | Maximize **liquidity**; zero-trust incentives |
| **Compute** | Hardware under models | Match **assurance** to sensitivity (structural > contractual) |

## 15 steps (titles only)

**Foundations:** ZDR · AI decision tree · Architecture review  

**Model:** Misaligned incentives · Model liquidity · Model flywheel  

**Compute:** Hardware by assurance · Adaptable hardware for sensitive work · Verify unowned compute  

**Control:** Model agnostic · Granular permissions · Audit & log · Adaptive cybersecurity · Build by branching · **Context flywheel**

---

## Terms that matter

- **ZDR** — zero data retention beyond ephemeral inference (necessary, not sufficient).  
- **EPM** — extraction-prone model: third-party incentive to compete *and* no ZDR → assume knowledge can leave.  
- **Model liquidity** — switch providers without losing the system.  
- **Context flywheel** — usage → structured signal → ontology → better workflows (you own this loop).  
- **Branching** — reversible agent action (fork before commit).
